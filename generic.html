Tidy Tides

Role: Level Design, Programmer - 2024

Tidy Tides is a game designed to promote ocean cleanliness by collecting and recycling trash. We have introduced an NPC who guides the player through the basics, and I have created an inventory system that can store items and drop them when needed. There are two types of items: trash and consumables, which restore health and oxygen. To add some constraints, we introduced a timer, requiring the player to collect the trash before it runs out. If the player fails, a shark will spawn, and upon collision, a turn-based combat system is triggered. When the player recycles the trash, they receive coins, which can be used at the shop to buy consumables.

Spring Knight

Role: Level Design, Programmer - 2022

Spring Knight is a challenging 2D platform-jumping game inspired by *Jump King*. It tests players' precision and problem-solving as they control a knight bouncing through increasingly difficult levels. The game offers simple yet addictive gameplay with an emphasis on mastering physics-based jumps. It has a very unique level design which can often frustrate the players with its pinpoint precise jumps.

Infinite Odyssey

Role: Programmer - 2023

Infinite Odyssey is a board game developed during a 48-hour game jam based on the theme "Loops." Players navigate a dynamic board using cards and dice, where every move loops back into gameplay decisions. The project combines fun mechanics with strategic elements to create a memorable party experience.
